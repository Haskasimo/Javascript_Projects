Welcome to the Sinopah

Our largest unit. This spacious, 1600 square foot unit has three large bedrooms, each with a private bathroom.

  • The Sunrise bedroom has a king bed, full closet, and bathroom with a tub/shower.

  • The Garden View room has two queen beds, a walk in closet, and bathroom with a tiled walk-in shower with a European glass door.

  • The Southwest bedroom has a king bed, a private bathroom with a tiled walk-in shower, and European glass door. All the bedrooms have ceiling fans to keep you cool and comfortable.

Enjoy the spacious great room, featuring a fully equipped kitchen with granite countertops, custom built cabinets, new appliances, and plenty of tools to prepare a meal for a large gathering of family or friends. Relax in the living room area with comfortable couches and an HDTV with a Rouku, so that you can stay connected to the outside world - if you wish. The dining table easily sits eight, and the kitchen island sits four more. The rock fireplace can be ablaze at the turn of the switch, giving you a cozy fire on cool evenings.
